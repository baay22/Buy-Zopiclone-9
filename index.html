Types of Sleeping Tablets

Sleeping tablets encompass a diverse array of medications, each tailored to address specific sleep-related issues.

Types of Sleep Medications:

  • Benzodiazepines: Drugs like diazepam and lorazepam induce relaxation and sleepiness by acting on the central nervous system.
  • Non-benzodiazepine hypnotics: Medications such as zolpidem and zaleplon target specific receptors in the brain to promote sleep.
  • Melatonin agonists: Drugs like ramelteon and tasimelteon mimic the effects of the hormone melatonin, regulating the sleep-wake cycle.
  • Antidepressants:Certain antidepressants like trazodone and amitriptyline may be used off-label to improve sleep, especially in individuals with depression or anxiety.
